What Information Does the Application Obtain and How Is It Used?

The Application may collect certain information automatically, including but not limited to: the type of mobile device you use, your device's unique ID, the IP address of your device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about how you use the Application.


Location Information and Geofencing

The Application may request access to your device’s location solely for the purpose of verifying that its use is not occurring in restricted geographic areas (e.g., warzones or other prohibited regions). The Application uses this geolocation check to determine compliance with applicable laws and usage restrictions.

Important details regarding location data:

  • Local Processing Only: Your location data is processed entirely on your device. The Application checks your device’s location against a list of restricted geographies stored within the Application itself.
  • No Storage or Transfer: The Application does not store your location coordinates, nor does it transmit location data off your device.
  • Permission-Based: You may choose not to grant the Application access to your device’s location. However, if you do not grant permission, you may be unable to use certain features that require verification of your location.
